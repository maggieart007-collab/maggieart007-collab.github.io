Alchemist's Essence is a story woven from the threads of past and present.

 

Inspired by ancient rituals, natural forces, and alchemical symbolism, the fragrance takes shape in a clean, contemporary design.

 

This project explores the balance between tactile materiality and visual lightness, between archetypes and modern relevance.

The AURUM MUNDI logo captures the spirit of alchemical exploration and high fashion aesthetics. The typeface merges precise geometry with subtle asymmetry, evoking a sense of modern mysticism. The custom D features an abstract alchemical diagram — a symbol of transformation, pursuit of perfection, and inner light. It's a visual metaphor for the golden ratio, enclosed within the silhouette of a perfume bottle.

Project Brief

Target Audience:

Women and men 28–45, high income, interested in self-development, spirituality, design and conscious consumption. They value authenticity, depth and aesthetics with meaning.

Core Idea:

Every bottle is a modern ritual that connects the wearer with their inner power and the ancient wisdom of transformation.

Positioning:

"Not just a perfume — an alchemical act of transformation."

Key Messages:

  • One touch, and the world subtly shifts.
  • Rooted in mystery. Designed for today.
  • From ancient rituals to modern luxury.

Impact & Results

This concept creates strong emotional differentiation in a highly competitive luxury segment.

The rich storytelling and symbolic visual system allow the brand to:

  • Justify premium pricing (expected $180–280 per bottle)
  • Build deeper customer loyalty through philosophy and ritual
  • Stand out both on the shelf and in digital space
  • Have high potential for extensions (candles, body care, fashion collaborations)

The project demonstrates the ability to turn visual aesthetics into a complete strategic brand concept.

Materials: frosted glass, wood, sand, marble
Bottle: minimalist shape
Mood: mystery, soft light, high fashion
